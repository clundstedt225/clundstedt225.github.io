About Me

I am a Programmer, Game Developer, and Student whose always had a love for computers, programming, and learning what it takes to create video games. Whether I was making maps for my favorite computer games or learning how to code, I have always been interested in learning any skills that will help me achieve this goal. While my main focus is on programming, I also enjoy other aspects of game development like 3D modeling, Animation, and Level Design.

I have personal as well as academic experience working in the Unity and Unreal game engines using C# and C++. I also have experience working on team projects using the agile methodology and more specifically the scrum framework.
